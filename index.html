About us

We are a team of Passionate photographers and want you to join us. We are all about sharing and collobarating with people with similiar interests. Coming together allows us to build a photography based on what we love doing, we can grow as a team by being able to provide feedback, look at other works whilest enhancing your photography skills.
This is an oppurtunity for you to grow as a photographer and build up a portfolio through small competitions each week which will help you get inspired. The weekly run competitions help to build up your portfolio. Private or public, you can vote, get inspired, partake, or just simply look, become part of photopassion!
There is a live feed to show the most popular works, the most recent, and previous winners, they take photos to help you and you can become part of this community, have your own account, the more photos, the more collobarating the better the site will be and the more passionate photographers will join.
